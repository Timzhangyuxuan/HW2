About Yuxuan

I'm a 23 years old student at the University of Iowa with Econ and MIS majors. I was born in the capital city of Shandong province, China. If you don't know where it is, imagine drawing a line between Beijing and Shanghai, and the middle point of the line is where I came from. 3 years ago, I travelled thousands of miles to the United States for education, cultural exploration as well as professional advancement. Now I am a senior student that is about to graduate in next May. I feel I will definitely miss this part of my life when I'm gone, although I don't know where I will go to.
